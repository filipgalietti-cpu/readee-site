♿ Accessibility

READEE is designed to be accessible to all learners

Our Commitment to Accessibility

READEE is committed to ensuring that all children, including those with disabilities, can access and benefit from our program. We continuously work to meet and exceed accessibility standards.

Accessibility Features

  • Screen Reader Compatible: Full compatibility with major screen reading software
  • Keyboard Navigation: Complete functionality without requiring a mouse
  • Adjustable Text Size: Users can increase or decrease text size as needed
  • High Contrast Mode: Option for high contrast display for visual accessibility
  • Closed Captions: All video content includes accurate closed captions
  • Audio Descriptions: Alternative text and audio descriptions for visual content

Standards Compliance

READEE strives to conform to Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA standards. We regularly audit our platform to identify and address accessibility barriers.

Accommodations for Learners

Our program can be adapted to support diverse learners:

  • Extended time for responses
  • Read-aloud functionality for all text
  • Visual supports and scaffolds
  • Reduced visual distractions
  • Multiple means of engagement and expression

Feedback and Support

We welcome feedback about accessibility. If you encounter barriers while using READEE or have suggestions for improvement, please contact us:

Email: accessibility@readee.org
