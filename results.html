Frequently Asked Questions

Can I change plans later?

Absolutely! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.

Is there a free trial?

Yes! The Recommended plan includes a 14-day free trial with no credit card required. Try it risk-free!

What ages does READEE support?

READEE is designed for children ages 4-10 (Pre-K through 3rd grade), with content aligned to early literacy standards.

Do you offer discounts for schools?

Yes! Contact our sales team for custom pricing for multiple classrooms or district-wide implementations.
