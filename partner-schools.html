School Partnerships

READEE partners with schools and districts across the country to support literacy instruction. Our partnerships range from individual classroom implementations to district-wide adoption.

What We Offer Schools

  • Free Access: No-cost program access for all students and teachers
  • Professional Development: Training for teachers on effective literacy instruction
  • Implementation Support: Guidance on integrating READEE into your literacy block
  • Progress Reporting: School and district-level data dashboards
  • Technical Support: Dedicated support team for schools
  • Customization: Alignment with your curriculum and standards

Partnership Models

Classroom Implementation

Individual teachers use READEE as a supplement to core instruction, particularly for intervention and differentiation.

Grade-Level Adoption

All teachers at a particular grade level use READEE to support consistent, effective literacy instruction across classrooms.

School-Wide Program

Entire elementary schools integrate READEE into their literacy framework, creating a coherent K-3 reading program.

District Partnership

Districts adopt READEE across multiple schools, with coordinated professional development and implementation support.

Current Partners

We're proud to partner with over 500 schools in 35 states, serving diverse communities from urban centers to rural areas. Our partner schools have seen:

  • 20-30% improvements in reading proficiency rates
  • Reduced achievement gaps for at-risk students
  • Increased teacher confidence in literacy instruction
  • Stronger home-school connections around reading

Interested in Partnering?

If your school or district is interested in partnering with READEE, we'd love to hear from you. Contact us to schedule a conversation about how we can support your literacy goals.
