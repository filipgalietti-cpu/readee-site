Answers to your most common questions about READEE

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about READEE below. Can't find what you're looking for? Contact us for personalized support.

Getting Started

Q: How do I get started with READEE?

A: Simply take our 2-minute assessment to determine your child's reading level, then create a free account to begin accessing lessons.

Q: Is READEE really free?

A: Yes! READEE is a nonprofit literacy program, and our basic program is completely free for families and educators.

Q: What ages is READEE designed for?

A: READEE is designed for children ages 4-10, typically pre-K through 3rd grade.

Using READEE

Q: How long should my child use READEE each day?

A: We recommend 10-15 minute sessions, 3-5 times per week. Consistency is more important than length.

Q: Can READEE replace my child's reading instruction at school?

A: No. READEE is designed to supplement, not replace, classroom instruction. It works best alongside your child's school reading program.

Q: What if my child finds it too difficult or too easy?

A: Our program adapts to your child's level. If lessons seem consistently too hard or too easy, retake the assessment or contact our support team.

Q: Do I need to sit with my child during lessons?

A: Initially, yes. As your child becomes familiar with the program and more independent, they can work more on their own.

Technical Questions

Q: What devices does READEE work on?

A: READEE works on computers, tablets, and smartphones with internet access and a modern web browser.

Q: Do we need headphones?

A: Headphones are helpful but not required. They can help minimize distractions and improve focus.

Q: Can multiple children use one account?

A: Yes! You can create separate profiles for each child within one account to track their individual progress.

For Educators

Q: Can I use READEE with my whole class?

A: Absolutely! READEE offers classroom management tools for teachers. Contact us for information about classroom accounts.

Q: Is READEE aligned with state standards?

A: Yes! Our curriculum is aligned with Common Core ELA standards and most state reading standards. See our standards page for details.

Q: Do you offer professional development for teachers?

A: Yes! We offer various professional learning opportunities for educators.
