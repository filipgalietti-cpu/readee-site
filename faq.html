Getting Started

How much does Readee cost?

Readee offers a free plan with the diagnostic assessment and your first 2 lessons per level. Readee+ is $9.99/month (or $79.99/year) for the full lesson library, up to 5 children, and detailed progress reports. Start with a free 7-day trial — no credit card required.

What ages is Readee designed for?

Readee is built for children ages 5–10 (K through 4th grade). Lessons adapt to your child's reading level.

How do I get started?

Take the free 2-minute diagnostic assessment to find your child's reading level, then create an account to start lessons right away.

Using Readee

How long does each lesson take?

About 10 minutes. Short enough to fit into any routine, long enough to build real skills. Consistency matters more than marathon sessions.

Is this good for kids with autism or learning differences?

Absolutely. Readee was designed with neurodiverse learners in mind. The structured, predictable format and calm visual design help children on the spectrum feel comfortable and focused.

How is this different from other reading apps?

Most reading apps are games that don't build real skills. Readee follows the Science of Reading — the same evidence-based approach used by the best literacy programs in schools.

Do I need to sit with my child during lessons?

Initially, yes. As your child becomes familiar with the program and more independent, they can work more on their own.

For Educators

Can I use Readee in my classroom?

Yes! Readee works great during centers, morning work, independent practice, or as structured homework. You'll get a teacher dashboard to track every student's progress. Setup takes under 5 minutes. Special educator pricing is available.

Is Readee aligned with state standards?

Yes! Our curriculum is aligned with Common Core ELA standards and most state reading standards.

Can multiple children use one account?

Yes! You can create separate profiles for each child within one account to track their individual progress. Readee+ supports up to 5 children.

Still have questions?

Reach out at hello@readee.app and we'll get back to you within 1–2 business days.
