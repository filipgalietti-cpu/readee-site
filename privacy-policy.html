How we protect and use your information

Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 11, 2026

At READEE, we take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it.

Information We Collect

We collect:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, and password for parent/teacher accounts
  • Student Information: First name, grade level, and learning data (non-personally identifiable)
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use READEE, including lesson completion and progress
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, and device information

How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide and improve our educational services
  • Personalize learning experiences
  • Communicate with users about their accounts and our services
  • Analyze and improve program effectiveness
  • Ensure security and prevent fraud

Student Privacy

We comply with COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act) and FERPA (Family Educational Rights and Privacy Act). We:

  • Never sell student data
  • Never use student data for advertising
  • Require parental consent for children under 13
  • Allow parents to review and delete their child's information

Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your information, including encryption, secure servers, and regular security audits.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data
  • Request corrections to your data
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Opt out of non-essential communications

Contact Us About Privacy

Questions about our privacy practices? Contact our privacy team:

Email: privacy@readee.org
