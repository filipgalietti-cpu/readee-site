Resources Designed for Educators

READEE provides a comprehensive collection of resources to support teachers in delivering effective literacy instruction. Our materials are research-based, classroom-tested, and aligned with best practices in reading education.

What's Available

  • Downloadable Teaching Guides: Step-by-step instructions for implementing READEE in your classroom
  • Printable Worksheets: Supplementary activities that reinforce key concepts
  • Assessment Tools: Diagnostic assessments to identify student needs and track progress
  • Parent Communication Templates: Ready-to-use letters and handouts to engage families
  • Differentiation Strategies: Guidance for meeting diverse learner needs
  • Extension Activities: Ideas for students who need additional challenge

Curriculum Integration

Our resources are designed to complement your existing literacy curriculum, not replace it. Whether you use a core reading program or a workshop approach, READEE materials can be seamlessly integrated into your instructional day.

Professional Learning Support

In addition to materials, we provide professional learning resources including:

  • Video tutorials demonstrating best practices
  • Research summaries on the science of reading
  • Implementation guides for different grade levels
  • Strategies for using data to inform instruction

Collaborative Planning Tools

We understand that effective literacy instruction requires thoughtful planning. Access our collaborative planning tools to:

  • Create weekly lesson plans aligned with READEE objectives
  • Share resources with your grade-level team
  • Track student progress across your classroom
  • Access suggested modifications for special populations
