Our Mission

At READEE, we believe every child deserves the opportunity to become a confident, skilled reader. Our mission is to provide high-quality, accessible literacy instruction that empowers young learners to unlock their full potential.

Why We Exist

Reading is the foundation of all learning. Yet millions of children struggle with literacy, limiting their academic success and future opportunities. We're working to change that.

READEE was created to bridge the gap between what research tells us about effective reading instruction and what's actually available to families and educators‚Äîespecially those in under-resourced communities.

Our Core Values

  • Accessibility: Every child deserves access to excellent literacy instruction, regardless of their family's income
  • Evidence-Based Practice: We ground everything we do in the science of reading
  • Collaboration: We partner with schools, teachers, and families‚Äînever compete with them
  • Equity: We work to ensure all children have the tools they need to succeed
  • Continuous Improvement: We're always learning and refining our approach

Our Impact

Since our founding, READEE has:

  • Served over 100,000 students across the country
  • Partnered with 500+ schools and districts
  • Helped thousands of struggling readers achieve grade-level proficiency
  • Provided professional development to 5,000+ educators

Looking Forward

We envision a future where no child falls behind in reading. Through innovation, partnership, and dedication to evidence-based practice, we're working every day to make that vision a reality.
