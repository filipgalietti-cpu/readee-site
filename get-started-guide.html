Everything you need to begin your READEE journey

Welcome to READEE!

Starting a new reading program can feel overwhelming, but we've made it simple. This guide will walk you through everything you need to know to get started successfully.

Step 1: Take the Assessment

Begin by taking our quick 2-minute assessment to understand your child's current reading level. This helps us personalize the learning experience from day one.

Start Assessment

Step 2: Set Up Your Account

Create your free account and set up your child's profile. You'll be able to:

  • Track progress over time
  • Customize learning goals
  • Access resources and reports
  • Connect with educators if desired

Step 3: Start Learning

Jump into your first lesson! Each session is just 10-15 minutes and includes:

  • Engaging phonics activities
  • Vocabulary building exercises
  • Comprehension practice
  • Immediate feedback and encouragement

Tips for Success

  • Be Consistent: Aim for 3-5 sessions per week
  • Create a Routine: Pick a regular time each day for READEE
  • Celebrate Progress: Acknowledge effort and improvement
  • Stay Positive: Keep sessions fun and pressure-free
  • Review Progress: Check the dashboard weekly to see growth

Need Help?

Our support team is here for you! Check out our FAQ page or Help Center for answers to common questions.
