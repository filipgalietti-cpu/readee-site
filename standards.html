How READEE Follows ELA Standards

Aligned with English Language Arts Standards for Early Learners (Pre-K through 3rd Grade)

READEE is carefully designed to support young learners at the beginning of their literacy journey. Our curriculum aligns with Common Core State Standards for English Language Arts (ELA) for Pre-K through 3rd Grade, ensuring that students develop the foundational reading, writing, speaking, and listening skills they need to succeed in their early education years and beyond.
