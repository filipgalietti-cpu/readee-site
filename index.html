How READEE Builds Reading Comprehension

Step 1

Understand the Words

Students learn key vocabulary and practice decoding so the text actually makes sense.

Result: Fewer breakdowns, smoother reading.

Step 2

Understand the Text

Students track what's happening with short "micro-checks" that keep them on the same page.

Result: Better recall of details and sequence.

Step 3

Think About the Meaning

Students answer simple why/how questions, make connections, and explain their thinking.

Result: Stronger comprehension and confidence.
