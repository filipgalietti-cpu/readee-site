Flashcards & Key Terms

Tap a card to flip between definitions and real-world examples.

Assets
Resources owned by a business that are expected to provide future benefits. Example: Cash, inventory.
Liabilities
Obligations owed to external parties. Example: Accounts payable, loans.
Accrual Accounting
Recognizing revenues and expenses when they are incurred, not when cash is exchanged.
